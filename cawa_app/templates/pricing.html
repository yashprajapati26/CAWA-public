Guidlines

Submission Guidlines

For Abstract

Word limit for abstract is approx. 300 words, including all the important key words, font size 12 , Times New Roman.
Sub theme should be mentioned along with the abstract.
Title of the paper, Author Name & Designation, Institutional affiliation, E-mail ID & contact number.

For Fullpapaer

Title of the paper, Author Name & Designation, Institutional affiliation, E-mail ID & contact number.
All manuscripts should be of approx. 3000 words.
References should be cited in APA style.
Title in Bold letters (Font size 14).
Font in Times New Roman (Font size 12).
Line spacing should be 1.5 .
Paper should be submitted in MS-Word format.
The full paper received will be checked for plagiarism using software.
If plagiarism exceeds 15%, the paper will be sent back to the authors for modification.

Publication

Paper selected by the review committee will be published in conference proceeding book with ISBN No.
Best research paper will be awarded in each category.

Conference Participation

Atleast one author needs to remain present during conference to get the certificate of paper presentation otherwise it will be treated as in absentia.

Call For Papers / Articles

Theoretical, Conceptual, Empirical papers & Research Articles which are original, not published elsewhere are invited in accordance with the themes & sub-themes of the conference.
