About Us

K.S.School of Business Management

K S School of Business Management is one of the first institutions in the country offering Five-year Integrated Management Programme under the aegis of Gujarat University since 1993. It rolled out its first Five-year Integrated MSc (Computer Applications and Information Technology) programme in 1998. The institute continues to attract the cream of students of Gujarat and other neighbouring states. Over these years it has nurtured budding talent and has contributed to the development of Management and IT professionals in the domestic and international platform.

Gujarat University

Gujarat University is the largest University in Gujarat since 1949 with a significant history of being remarkably instrumental in the dynamic progress of the State through its advanced Higher Education programmes. Gujarat University recently completed 70 years of glorious existence and over these years has to its credit one of the largest bases of alumni. Gujarat University has done various MOUs & associations with research organizations, government agencies, industrial bodies to support students and faculties in order to create useful research for the society. With a robust network of around 47 PG departments in the campus, the University has taken necessary measures and initiatives to contribute to the advancement of research, academics and innovation in the country while keeping itself abreast with the times by launching various innovative programmes in the contemporary areas like forensic science, forensic accounting, climate change, biodiversity management, SEZ management, port management.

About Conference

KSCON is initiated by K.S .School of Business Management to ignite intellectual minds of the academia, researchers & students to discuss the emerging trends in management & IT in the present era.
Business in modern times is characterized by uncertainty, complexity and agility which presents a plethora of opportunities in terms of developing innovative business practices in the field of Management and Information Technology.
In the present scenario, KS Conference aims at contemplating over these trending issues and invites paper presentations from academia, industry, research scholars and the student community.
